Instruction 1:
Art style：Pixel art
Subject：Chinese architecture and countryside scenery
Details：Detailed depiction of traditional Chinese buildings, such as temples, pagodas, and pavilions; lush green fields with crops and flowers; serene rivers or lakes with small bridges; dense forests with tall trees and winding paths.
Composition：Balanced composition with the main focus on the architecture and prominent features of the scenery. Use leading lines to guide the viewer's eyes through the scene.
Lighting and shadows：Soft and natural lighting to create a peaceful atmosphere. Use shadows to add depth and dimension to the elements.
Elements：Include traditional Chinese elements like red lanterns, traditional Chinese roofs, and decorative patterns. Incorporate elements of nature like birds, butterflies, and flowing water.
Color palette：Use a warm and harmonious color palette with earthy tones and pops of vibrant colors for the details.
Camera angle：Choose a slightly elevated perspective to showcase the overall scenery and emphasize the architecture.
Other details：Pay attention to small details like intricate carvings on the buildings, reflections in the water, and subtle variations in foliage.

指令内容：
表现形式：像素艺术
创作风格：古风像素艺术
主体：中国传统建筑以及田园山水风景
细节刻画：详细描绘传统的中国建筑，如寺庙、塔楼和亭台；绿油油的田野上有庄稼和鲜花；宁静的河流或湖泊上有小桥；茂密的森林里有高大的树木和曲折的小路。
构图：平衡的构图，以建筑和风景的突出特点为主要焦点。使用引导线引导观众的目光穿过整个场景。
光影：柔和自然的光线营造宁静的氛围。利用阴影给元素增添深度和层次感。
画面元素：包括红灯笼、传统的中国屋顶和装饰图案等中国传统元素。结合鸟类、蝴蝶和流动的水等自然元素。
色调：采用温暖和谐的色调，以土色调为主，并在细节处使用鲜艳的颜色点缀。
镜头角度：选择稍微提高的视角，展示整体风景并强调建筑物。
其他细节：注意建筑物上精细的雕刻、水中的倒影和植被的细微变化。

Instruction 2:
Art style：Pixel art
Subject：Chinese ink painting-inspired scenery
Details：Simplify the elements and focus on capturing the essence of traditional Chinese ink paintings. Use bold and expressive brushstrokes to depict mountains, rivers, and trees. Include iconic Chinese landmarks such as the Great Wall or a traditional Chinese garden.
Composition：Create a sense of harmony and balance in the composition, following the principles of traditional Chinese painting. Use empty spaces to evoke a sense of tranquility and contemplation.
Lighting and shadows：Use minimalistic and suggestive lighting to create a dreamy atmosphere. Use subtle shadows to add depth and dimension to the scenery.
Elements：Incorporate symbolic elements like cherry blossoms, bamboo, or lotus flowers. Include elements of Chinese culture like calligraphy or traditional Chinese characters.
Color palette：Use a limited color palette inspired by traditional Chinese ink paintings, with shades of black, gray, and muted earth tones.
Camera angle：Experiment with various perspectives, including close-ups and panoramic views, to create different visual experiences for the players.
Other details：Pay attention to capturing the fluidity and movement of ink paintings, as well as the texture and brushwork.

指令内容：
表现形式：像素艺术
创作风格：受中国水墨画启发的风景
主体：以简化元素为主，专注于捕捉中国水墨画的精髓。运用大胆而富有表现力的笔触描绘山水和树木。包括中国的标志性地标，如长城或传统的中国花园。
细节刻画：在构图上追求和谐和平衡，遵循中国传统绘画的原则。利用留白来唤起宁静和沉思的感觉。
光影：使用简约而富有暗示性的照明，营造梦幻般的氛围。利用微妙的阴影为风景增添深度和层次感。
画面元素：包括樱花、竹子或荷花等象征性元素。加入中国文化元素，如书法或传统的汉字。
色调：受中国传统水墨画的启发，采用有限的色调，包括黑色、灰色和柔和的土色调。
镜头角度：尝试不同的视角，包括特写和全景视图，为玩家创造不同的视觉体验。
其他细节：注意捕捉水墨画的流动感和运动感，以及纹理和笔触的表现。

Instruction 3:
Art style：Pixel art
Subject：Chinese fantasy world
Details：Create a vibrant and magical world inspired by Chinese mythology and folklore. Include mythical creatures like dragons, phoenixes, and unicorns. Incorporate elements of traditional Chinese architecture with a fantastical twist, such as floating palaces or pagodas.
Composition：Experiment with asymmetrical and dynamic compositions to evoke a sense of adventure and excitement. Use diagonal lines and sharp angles to create a sense of movement and energy.
Lighting and shadows：Use vibrant and fantastical lighting to enhance the magical atmosphere. Play with dramatic shadows to add depth and drama to the scene.
Elements：Include mystical artifacts, ancient symbols, and celestial motifs in the scenery. Incorporate elements of Chinese martial arts, such as martial artists or flying warriors.
Color palette：Use a bold and saturated color palette with a mix of vibrant and pastel colors to create a whimsical and enchanting atmosphere.
Camera angle：Experiment with various perspectives, including low angles and bird's-eye views, to showcase the grandeur and scale of the fantasy world.
Other details：Pay attention to intricate details in the mythical creatures, architectural ornaments, and magical effects.

指令内容：
表现形式：像素艺术
创作风格：中国幻想世界
主体：受中国神话和民间故事启发，创造一个充满活力和神奇的世界。包括龙、凤凰和独角兽等神话生物。将传统的中国建筑元素与奇幻的扭曲相结合，如飘浮的宫殿或塔楼。
细节刻画：尝试不对称和动态的构图，唤起冒险和兴奋的感觉。使用对角线和锐角来创造运动感和能量感。
光影：使用鲜艳而奇幻的照明来增强神奇的氛围。运用戏剧性的阴影为场景增添深度和戏剧感。
画面元素：在风景中包括神秘的神器、古老的符号和天体图案。融入中国武术元素，如武术者或飞行的战士。
色调：采用鲜明和饱和的色调，混合了鲜艳和柔和的色彩，营造出奇幻而迷人的氛围。
镜头角度：尝试不同的视角，包括低角度和俯视角度，展示幻想世界的壮丽和规模。
其他细节：注意神话生物、建筑装饰物和魔法效果中的精细细节。
